== Description ==

SEO, Privacy, Encryption, Redirect, Invest and Affiliate Link Cloaker of URL inside your posts. Build in between page with delay sometime to show your ADs before directing visitors to trargeted page.

<br> 
Features:<br>

-Make long ugly affiliate links turn into short "hijack proof" url's that any one can remember<br>

-Improve your SEO by hide out-links from your posts<br>

-URL Encryption <br>

-Automatically process Redirect of URLs <br>

-Showing your own ADs before directing visitors to targeted page <br>

-Retain pagerank of your site by giving links out parameter that makes Googlebot think it was looking at a session id link and ignore it.<br>

-Do `Double Meta Refresh (DMR)` method to prevent your site from appearing in the server logs of referred pages as referrer  <br>

-If (DMR) faild to hide a referrer then use public Anonymize links `anonym.to`.<br>

-Process both pages and posts <br>

== Installation ==

1. Unzip and Upload the folder 'url-cloarker' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Settings and configure your Ads code in the options page.
